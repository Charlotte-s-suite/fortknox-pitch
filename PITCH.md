# Fort Knox of Alameda — the Merritt Digital pitch
_Companion to the live draft: **charlotte-s-suite.github.io/fortknox-pitch** · 2026-07-02_

## The case, in three demonstrations

Open their current site and ours side by side on a phone. Then say three things:

1. **"Your site doesn't fit on a phone."** Their page drags out of frame on mobile — an old
   slider component wider than the screen. Most of their customers find them on a phone.
   *(Demonstrate live on their own device — it reproduces every time.)*
2. **"Your browser calls you 'Not Secure'."** The site has no HTTPS. For a business whose brand
   is literally a *safe, secure environment* — named FORT KNOX — the address bar contradicts the
   sign above the door. Ours ships encrypted by default.
3. **"You send customers away to see the gold price."** Their homepage links *out* to another
   site for spot prices — the single piece of information every customer wants. Ours shows
   **live gold and silver spot on the page** (working in the draft right now). That turns the
   site from a brochure into something people check daily — and every check is a visit.

## The design story (30 seconds)

> "We didn't design you a website. We designed you an **assay card** — the packaging your best
> product already comes in."

The draft borrows the visual language of the PAMP Suisse gold bar card: deep card-stock navy,
banknote-grade engraved filigree bleeding off the edge, white presentation windows that make the
precious thing glow, letterspaced gold serif — F O R T  K N O X the way P A M P sits. Swiss
restraint: gold only where it's precious, ornament with the confidence of currency.

## What the draft already does

- **Live spot ticker** — real prices, fetched on load, in the white assay window
- **Six department cards** — gold, coins, estate jewelry, diamonds, watches, sterling — each an
  assay window with engraved-line icons
- **The appraisal as the product** — "Private. Unhurried. In your presence, every step." with
  booking as the single conversion (tap-to-call + appointment request)
- **The local story** — "just over the Park Street bridge," map, hours, hallmark trust badges
- **Mobile-first** — verified pixel-exact at iPhone width; zero horizontal overflow
- One static file. No plugins to rot, nothing to hack, loads instantly.
