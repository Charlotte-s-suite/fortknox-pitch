About

A family business, over the counter, for more than thirty years.

For over thirty years, Fort Knox Rare Coin & Bullion has been Alameda's trusted, family-run buyer and seller of precious metals, coins and estate treasures — Michael Sr., Linda, and their son Michael, all expert, certified coin graders, working with you across the counter.

Scrap gold & silver

We buy any gold or silver item, regardless of condition. We are always in need of gold chains, school rings, dental gold, wedding rings, and anything marked sterling silver.

Coins & paper money

We buy and sell all United States paper money — bring it in for a generous offer, with no obligation on your part. We also purchase foreign coins, especially those with any gold or silver content.

Diamonds

Whether you are buying or selling, contact us for the best possible price. We are always in need of diamond rings and loose gemstones — and we can supply a stone in any size and quality you desire.

Investment programs

To acquire investment-quality coins, call and make an appointment with Michael Sr., Linda, or our son Michael to discuss your numismatic needs. We are all expert, certified coin graders.

Want lists

We are part of a networked computer system of approximately 1,500 dealers nationwide. If we don't have the item you're looking for, we'll gladly locate it for you at a very competitive price. Drop in to fill out a want-list card, or send us your list in the mail.

Finder's fees

If you locate a numismatic coin collection or diamonds which we purchase, we will reward you a 3% finder's fee.

Publications & supplies

We carry a range of useful references — Numismatic News, Coin World, the Blue Book (the Handbook of U.S. Coins) and the Red Book (the Guide of U.S. Coins) — along with Whitman coin folders and other collector supplies.
