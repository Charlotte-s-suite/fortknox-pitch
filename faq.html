Good to know

Questions

The things people ask us most — answered plainly, before you visit.

How long has Fort Knox of Alameda been in business?

Fort Knox has been serving the Bay Area for over 30 years and counting.

What types of precious metals do you buy and sell?

Fort Knox deals in precious metals including gold, silver, platinum and palladium.

Does Fort Knox buy coins, including rare coins?

Fort Knox buys all U.S. gold, silver, type and token coins. We are also big buyers of rare coins and can handle any collection you might have.

Can Fort Knox help me identify the value of items I want to sell?

If you don't know the market for an item you want to sell — if there is one — we will find it for you.

What types of gold items does Fort Knox buy?

Fort Knox buys any form of gold: gold coins from any country, used or unwanted gold, rings, chains, bracelets, necklaces, dental gold, gold dust, and more.

What types of silver items does Fort Knox buy?

Fort Knox buys .999 silver coins, .925 sterling coins, and sterling silver flatware.

Is there a display of jewelry at the store?

We do not display jewelry in our store.

Can Fort Knox help me find precious stones I'm looking for?

Fort Knox can provide you with a stone in any size or quality that you desire.

Does Fort Knox do appraisals?

Fort Knox can give you a verbal appraisal on your items, and can also provide a written appraisal upon request. If you simply need to know whether something is real, we can tell you that too.

Will I be paid cash for items I sell to Fort Knox?

Fort Knox will pay cash if you request it and if we have it on hand.

Can I make a special appointment outside normal hours?

If you cannot make it to Fort Knox of Alameda during our normal hours of operation, feel free to request a special appointment.

Bottom line: Fort Knox of Alameda is very knowledgeable, and we'll do our best to assist you in any way we can. We love our referrals.
